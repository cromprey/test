Thoughts on HTML's Utility

HTML allowed widespread access to the Internet and its possibilities. It turned digital technology into a tool for mass communication. HTML is the standard markup language for the creation of websites. The possibilities with HTML are extraordinary. You can do all sorts of things with HTML. It is what runs most of our internet. Which is why it is so important to learn in this class.

You can structure websites with HTML. With tags and elements you can define headings, paragraphs, and other content of a web page. You can also navigate the internet. Every website and internet browser is made up of HTML. For embedding images and videos you will need HTML. Also game development is made up of HTML. HTML is made into almost everything on the internet. That is why it is so important and the possibilities with it are incredible.
